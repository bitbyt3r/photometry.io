Introduction

Photometric Stereo is a method of acquiring depth images and 3D reconstructions from a single viewpoint of a scene captured under varying lighting conditions. Photogrammetry is a separate class of methods that generate a 3D reconstruction using multiple viewpoints under a single lighting condition. Pygmalion is a piece of software that combines these methods along with a general camera control framework to make 3D reconstruction more accessible and reliable.

Pygmalion is still very much a work in progress.
